# Nexly Privacy Policy **Last Updated: July 13, 2026** --- ## 1. Introduction Welcome to Nexly. Nexly is a Discord server management and automation platform developed and maintained by Nexly Labs. This Privacy Policy explains how Nexly collects, uses, stores, and protects information when users interact with our Discord bot, website, dashboard, and related services. By using Nexly, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy. --- ## 2. Information We Collect Nexly may collect and process the following information: ### Discord Account Information When you use Nexly or connect to our dashboard through Discord, we may process information provided by Discord, including: * Discord User ID * Username and display information provided by Discord * Discord account information required for authentication * Server permissions required to verify access to server management features ### Discord Server Information To provide server management and automation features, Nexly may process: * Discord Server ID * Server name * Channel IDs * Role IDs * Server configuration settings created by administrators * Information required for Nexly features to function correctly ### Dashboard Information If you use the Nexly web dashboard, we may process information received through Discord OAuth2 authentication, including information necessary to identify you and determine which servers you can manage. Nexly does not receive or store your Discord password. --- ## 3. How We Use Information Nexly uses collected information to: * Provide and maintain bot functionality * Provide server management and automation features * Authenticate users in the web dashboard * Store server configuration settings * Improve reliability and performance * Detect and prevent abuse or misuse of the service * Provide technical support Nexly only processes information necessary to provide its features. --- ## 4. Information We Do Not Collect Unless explicitly stated in future updates, Nexly does not: * Collect Discord passwords * Store Discord authentication tokens * Sell personal information * Use personal information for advertising purposes * Read or store private messages Nexly only accesses information required for its intended functionality. --- ## 5. Data Storage and Security Nexly takes reasonable measures to protect stored information from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse. Access to internal systems is limited to authorized members of Nexly Labs who require access to maintain and improve the service. While we work to protect user information, no online service can guarantee complete security. --- ## 6. Data Retention Nexly stores information only for as long as necessary to provide its services. Server administrators may request deletion of stored server-related data by contacting Nexly Labs. Users may request deletion of personal information associated with their use of Nexly where applicable. --- ## 7. Third-Party Services Nexly relies on third-party services necessary for providing its functionality, including: * Discord services and APIs * Hosting and infrastructure providers These services may process information according to their own privacy policies. Nexly is not affiliated with Discord Inc. --- ## 8.
