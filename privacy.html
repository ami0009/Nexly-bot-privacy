Nexly Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 13, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Nexly.

Nexly is a Discord server management and automation platform developed and maintained by Nexly Labs. This Privacy Policy explains how Nexly collects, uses, stores, and protects information when users interact with our Discord bot, website, dashboard, and related services.

By using Nexly, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect

Nexly may collect and process the following information:

Discord Account Information

  • Discord User ID
  • Username and display information provided by Discord
  • Discord account information required for authentication
  • Server permissions required to verify access to management features

Discord Server Information

  • Discord Server ID
  • Server name
  • Channel IDs
  • Role IDs
  • Server configuration settings created by administrators
  • Information required for Nexly features to function correctly

Dashboard Information

If you use the Nexly web dashboard, we may process information received through Discord OAuth2 authentication, including information necessary to identify you and determine which servers you can manage.

Nexly does not receive or store your Discord password.

3. How We Use Information

Nexly uses collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain bot functionality
  • Provide server management and automation features
  • Authenticate users in the web dashboard
  • Store server configuration settings
  • Improve reliability and performance
  • Detect and prevent abuse or misuse of the service
  • Provide technical support

4. Information We Do Not Collect

Unless explicitly stated in future updates, Nexly does not:

  • Collect Discord passwords
  • Store Discord authentication tokens
  • Sell personal information
  • Use personal information for advertising purposes
  • Read or store private messages

5. Data Storage and Security

Nexly takes reasonable measures to protect stored information from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse.

Access to internal systems is limited to authorized members of Nexly Labs who require access to maintain and improve the service.

While we work to protect user information, no online service can guarantee complete security.

6. Data Retention

Nexly stores information only for as long as necessary to provide its services.

Server administrators may request deletion of stored server-related data by contacting Nexly Labs.

Users may request deletion of personal information associated with their use of Nexly where applicable.

7. Third-Party Services

Nexly relies on third-party services necessary for providing its functionality, including:

  • Discord services and APIs
  • Hosting and infrastructure providers

These services may process information according to their own privacy policies.

Nexly is not affiliated with Discord Inc.

8. GDPR and User Rights

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), you may have rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), including:

  • The right to request access to your personal data
  • The right to request correction of inaccurate data
  • The right to request deletion of your data
  • The right to object to certain processing activities

To exercise these rights, contact Nexly Labs using the contact information below.

9. Children's Privacy

Nexly is intended to be used by individuals who are permitted to use Discord according to Discord's Terms of Service.

Nexly does not knowingly collect personal information from users who are not permitted to use Discord.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

Nexly Labs may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in the service, technology, or legal requirements.

The latest version will always be available through the official Nexly website.
